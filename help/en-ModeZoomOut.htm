Zoom Out

Contents=>Modes=>View=>Zoom Out

Tool bar:

Menu: View|Zoom Out

Cursor:

Used to reduce the image.

1. Specify the new center point of the window. At the same time the image redraws, the specified point is in the center of the window, and all the image is halved.

2. Repeat step 1 until the desired reduction is achieved.

3. To stop the reduction, press the right mouse button.

Important! This mode is stack mode, i.e. for the duration of this mode the previous mode is interrupted, and after the termination of this mode the previous mode is restored in the same place where it was interrupted.

  • You can specify a point by clicking the left mouse button or manually.
  • The termination of the input is done by pressing the right mouse button.
