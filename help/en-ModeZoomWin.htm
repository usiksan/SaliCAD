Zoom window

Содержание=>Режимы=>Вид=>Zoom window

Tool bar:

Menu: View|Zoom window

Cursor:

Serves to zoom in so that the entire specified area it fits right on the whole screen.

1. Enter first corner of rectangular window.

2. Specify the second corner of the rectangular window. At this time the view repaints, and the scale is scaled in such a way that the entire the specified window is placed exactly on the entire screen.

3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 until the desired scale is achieved.

4. To stop the mode, press the right mouse button.

Important! This mode is stack mode, i.e. for the duration of this mode the previous mode is interrupted, and after the termination of this mode the previous mode is restored in the same place where it was interrupted.

  • You can specify a point by clicking the left mouse button or manually.
  • The termination of the input is done by pressing the right mouse button.
