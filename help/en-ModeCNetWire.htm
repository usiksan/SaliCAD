Insert net wire

Contents=>Modes=>Sheet specific modes=>Insert net wire

Tool bar:

Menu: Sheet|Insert net wire

Cursor:

Inserts net wire into schematic sheet.

1. Setup enter net wire name

2. Setup/change net wire properties

3. Enter start point of segment

4. Enter end point of the segment. After you specify the end point of the segment, it is entered into the project. Automatically, the end point of the segment serves as the starting point of the next segment, thus allowing the formation of a polyline.

5. Repeat steps 2-4 to enter the entire polyline.

6. To stop entering the net wire polyline, press the right mouse button.

Note: If the next point of the net wire polyline falls on a segment of another net wire polyline, you will be prompted to combine the two chains into one. If both net wires have names different from the default names, the dialog of the option of combining net wires is additionally displayed.

Net wire name and properties set specified with net wire properties bar:

  • You can specify a point by clicking the left mouse button or manually.
  • The termination of the input is done by pressing the right mouse button.

"Smart mode"

There are two levels of smart mode for net wire mode:

At the first level, when you enter the first point, smart mode offers a full connection in the vicinity of the mouse cursor. The starting point can be offered both on the unconnected pin and on the other net segment.

Net wire "smart mode" example. System offers to connect pin A to pin B.

Net wire "smart mode" example. System offers to connect existing net wire segment A to pin B.

At the second level, when entering the next, but not the first point of the net wire polyline, this point is offered at the nearest free pin at the last movement of the cursor direction. In other words, the search for free pin is in the direction of the mouse cursor movement. At the same time, a polyline is proposed before this conclusion.

Middle mouse button or "space" - confirmation of "smart" input.
