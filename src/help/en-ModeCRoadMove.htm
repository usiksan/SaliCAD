Road move

Contents=>Modes=>Plate modes=>Road move

Tool bar:

Menu: Plate|Road move

Cursor:

This mode used for moving traced roads on the plate, i.e. for topology change.

1. Move your mouse over the road segment. The catcher icon will show which part of the segment will move. The square icon indicates that the end of the segment will move. The rhombus icon indicates that the entire segment will be moved.

2. Press the left mouse button and without releasing it move the mouse to form a new topology geometry. When you move, trace rules are controlled. The geometry of a segment changes to reflect the topology of adjacent segments. The move could be blocked if you violate the rules of the trace, and also if you cannot move, for example, if the segment is connected to the component pin.

3. Repeat steps 1-2 to edit the topology of all the desired segments.

  • You can specify a point by clicking the left mouse button or manually.
  • The termination of the input is done by pressing the right mouse button.

"Smart" mode is not present.
