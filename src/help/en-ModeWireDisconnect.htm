Disconnect net

Contents=>Modes=>Sheet specific modes=>Disconnect net

Tool bar:

Menu: Sheet|Disconnect net

Cursor:

Disconnects the pin from the net (or the net from the pin). When moving components, the nets connected to the pins are extended, shortened or broken depending on the movement, but the connection between the pin and the net is not broken. In cases where it is undesirable to disconnect the net from the pins of the component.

1. Specify the pin from which you want to disconnect the net.

2. Repeat step 1 to disconnect all of the required nets.

It is also possible to disconnect the group of pins at once. To do this, press the left mouse button and hold it to cover the group of disconnected pins with a rectangle. When you release the left mouse button, all the pins that fall into the enclosing rectangle are disconnected from the nets.

  • You can specify a point by clicking the left mouse button or manually.
  • The termination of the input is done by pressing the right mouse button.

"Smart mode" is not present
