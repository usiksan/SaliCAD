Select and edit

Contents=>Modes=>Select and edit

Tool bar:

Menu: Draw|Select and edit

Cursor:

Use this mode to select, copy, move, and delete objects. When you move the cursor over the surface of the graphical editor window, its appearance changes depending on whether the cursor is over the object or not. If the cursor is not over the object, it has the form . If the cursor is over an object, its appearance also depends on whether the object is selected or not. If the cursor is over an object and the object is not selected, the cursor takes the form . If the cursor is over the selected object, it has the form , in addition, if the cursor is over the selected object and the Ctrl key is pressed, the cursor has the form .

Object selection:

To select an object you need to click on it (the cursor has the form ) the left mouse button.

To select a group of objects it is necessary not over the object (the cursor has a form ) to press the left mouse button and without releasing it to cover with a dashed rectangle the group of objects to be selected, thus the cursor has a form . Release the left mouse button when the coverage is complete.

To select other objects in addition to the selected objects, hold down the Shift key. Additional objects are selected either individually (the cursor has a form ) or as a group, and the cursor has a form .

When objects are selected, their current properties are displayed in the corresponding properties panel, which changes depending on the type of selected elements. The properties of the selected elements can be changed directly in the properties panel. At the same time, property changes are instantly reflected on the selected objects. If objects with different property bars are selected, the property bar is displayed only for the same objects. At the same time, changes made from the properties panel to these objects do not affect other objects.

To remove the selection of objects you need to click the mouse over the object.

You can move, copy, and delete selected objects.

To delete the selected objects, press the Delete key.

To copy the selected objects, press and hold the Ctrl key, click the left mouse button over the selected objects and, without releasing the left mouse button, specify the new location of the copies of objects. Objects can be copied both within a single window and between the Windows of one or more projects. When you copy, the cursor is in the form .

To move the selected objects, click the left mouse button above the selected objects and, without releasing the left mouse button, specify the new location of the objects. When you move the cursor, it looks like .

When transferring and copying various objects, there are some features:

  • when transferring lines, if the selection was carried out for the tip of the segment, then this tip is transferred, the other tip remains in place, and the segment is deformed accordingly. This allows you to quickly change the length and direction of the segments;
  • when you move rectangles, if the selection was made at the corner of the rectangle, then only this angle is transferred, and the opposite remains in place, and the rectangle is deformed accordingly. This allows you to quickly resize rectangles;
  • copying identifiers and values is not possible at all;
  • when you copy the legs of the character numerical indexes in the names of the legs are increased by 1;
  • when copying the legs of the body digital indexes in the numbers of the legs are increased by 1;
  • separate copying of the name or number of the leg is not possible;
  • the anchor point is not transferred or copied;
  • when transferring chain segments, if the selection of the chain was carried out for the tip, then only this tip is transferred, the other tip remains in place, and the segment is deformed in the orthogonal direction; when transferring segments associated with it, the segments are deformed accordingly in orthogonal directions, the associated components are also transferred;
  • when you move components, the associated chain segments are deformed in orthogonal directions accordingly.

Selected objects can be rotated around a point. For rotation is a mode of rotation.
