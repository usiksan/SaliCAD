Tool bar:

Menu: Draw|Filled rect

Cursor:

1. Setup rectangle edges attributes

2. Enter first vertex of rectangle

3. (Optional) Change rectangle edges attributes

4. Enter second vertex of rectangle

  • You can specify a point by clicking the left mouse button or manually.
  • The termination of the input is done by pressing the right mouse button.
