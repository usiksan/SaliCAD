Filled circle enter mode

Contents=>Modes=>Draw modes=>Filled circle

Tool bar:

Menu: Draw|Filled circle

Cursor:

1. Setup circle attributes.

2. Enter circle center point.

3. Enter any circle point to fix diameter.

  • You can specify a point by clicking the left mouse button or manually.
  • The termination of the input is done by pressing the right mouse button.
